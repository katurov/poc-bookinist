## Gault&Millau's review

You will meet the spirit of Vojvodina in its most beautiful form in Nasuvo i friško. The menu is dominated by traditional dishes. Nasuvo, homemade pasta, in several varieties - with potatoes, cheese and bacon, cabbage, poppy seeds or crunchy nuts and breadcrumbs. They are accompanied by old-fashioned garnished sandwiches, as well as dishes with witty names uch as Paorski breakfast or Dođoski omelette with Njeguš prosciutto. There are also šufnudle, pierogi, shank with horseradish sauce, traditional cherry sauce, donuts and floating islands, and a reminder of the tastes of childhood - bread, fat, salt and pepper. A restaurant with a striking Vojvodina identity and warmth.

#### [ĆEVAPLIJA](/en/restaurants/%C4%86evaplija)

#### [POTKOVICA](/en/restaurants/restoran-potkovica) 

Through a quaint courtyard with wooden tables, you enter a rustic space that has remained a local favorite for decades. Renowned for its warm hospitality and horsemeat specialties, the restaurant is defined by a loyal staff that infuses the space with a homely, relaxed spirit. #### [DVA JELENA](/en/restaurants/dva-jelena) 

A cornerstone of Skadarlija, this legendary kafana is a definitive symbol of bohemian Belgrade. For decades, locals and travelers have gathered here to experience the city’s spirit through traditional cuisine and evocative old-town music. The rustic, multi-level interior exudes a storied warmth, beginning with artisanal appetizers - cured meats, local cheeses, and creamy kajmak - paired with house-baked bread. Robust mains feature time-honored classics, from grilled and slow-roasted meat under the sač to the signature hunter and Karađorđe steak. #### [BURGER HOUSE BROS](/en/restaurants/burger-house)

#### [TAVERNA PIATAKIA](/en/restaurants/taverna-piatakia)

#### [ĆEVAP KOD DEKIJA](/en/restaurants/%C4%86evap-kod-dekija)

#### [PIZZA FABRIKA](/en/restaurants/pizza-fabrika) 

Infused with the charm of Vračar and rooted in Italian tradition, Pizza Fabrika has spent years perfecting a focused concept: wood-fired pizza crafted from premium ingredients. The signature thin, crispy-edged dough serves as the ideal canvas for the Gustosa pizza with Parma ham or the indulgent Mia featuring mozzarella, gorgonzola, and mascarpone. Cheese aficionados frequently opt for the decadent six-cheese variation, a local favorite. Pizzas are elevated when paired with a classic Aperol Spritz, while the menu extends to artisanal bruschetta, focaccia, and hot sandwiches. The relaxed, unpretentious ambiance makes it the quintessential neighborhood spot where you drop in for a high-quality, effortless meal.

 

#### [KONOBA KOD GOCE I RENATA](/en/restaurants/konoba-kod-goce-i-renata) 

One of the favorite destinations to get away from the city and relax by the river, this tavern is a prime example of hospitality. The garden overlooking the Danube, and the rustic interior create a pleasant and intimate atmosphere. For starters, try the rich and spicy fish soup, homemade fish pate and crispy zucchini, bruschetta or the winter board. The friendly staff will recommend the catch of the day, catfish, perch, catfish and carp - fried or grilled, and there is also fish stew and goulash. They are known for grilled calamari, sea bream and sea bass, or shrimp and seafood combined with pasta. If meat is your choice, the selection from the grill, along with steak and pork chops, will surely satisfy you.

 

#### [BISTRO DU VIN](/en/restaurants/bistro-du-vin) 

Bistro du Vin follows Vračar’s daily rhythm from morning to evening, serving breakfast and weekend brunch. Standouts include the omelette, a superbly executed Croque Madame - ham, cheese, béchamel, and poached egg - light French toast, and both savory and sweet croissants. Later in the day, the menu expands considerably: club sandwiches, meat-topped salads, spaghetti, creamy gnocchi with shrimp, tuna and beef tartare, risotto, and the Bistro burger. For wine pairings - the list exceeds 150 labels - bruschetta, charcuterie and cheese boards, or baked Camembert with jam are reliable choices. A pleasant summer terrace adds further appeal to this well-rounded neighborhood bistro.

 

#### [JB PIVNICA](/en/restaurants/jb-pivnica) 

Pivnica is a premier gathering spot for those seeking a relaxed, convivial atmosphere paired with authentic, hearty cuisine. The menu focuses on traditional Serbian staples designed for a full table, beginning with a robust meze of artisanal cheeses, creamy kajmak, cured meats, and prebranac. Signature mains highlight regional specialties like the Pirot schnitzel – stuffed with local cheese - alongside classic Wiener Schnitzel and Karađorđe steak. A rotation of time-honored dishes, sarma, pilaf, pork stew and slow-boiled beef ensures a varied daily offering. Accompanied by house-baked buns, crisp salads, and a diverse selection of local beers, rakija, and wines, every meal is a celebrated local tradition.

 

#### [RESTORAN CANTINA DE FRIDA](/en/restaurants/restoran-cantina-de-frida) 

Cantina de Frida is a vibrant bar-restaurant, known for its energetic atmosphere and informal, urban approach to gastronomy. The cuisine is predominantly based on Mexican and Mediterranean traditions, with recognizable influences from Spain and South American flavors, interpreted in a modern and accessible way. The colorful menu includes classics such as guacamole, tacos, enchiladas and churros, but also more serious plates such as octopus, ribs and steaks. Offering an extensive selection of drinks and cocktails, and an entertainment program hosted nearly every evening, Cantina de Frida naturally establishes itself as a place where good food, music and nightlife meet in the same rhythm.

 

#### [CRNA OVCA](/en/restaurants/crna-ovca) 

Artisanal ice cream is crafted with care here, spanning a wide flavor range- from dependable classics to inventive and authentic combinations. Alongside year-round standards, expect rotating offerings such as poppy seed, walnut, mascarpone, raspberry with toasted pistachios, banana-lemon, and honey. The menu extends well beyond scoops: fruit ice cream on a stick, cookies, chocolate drinks, and cookie dough in a jar. Seasonal shifts in the flavor lineup make this a rewarding destination throughout the year, not merely in warmer months. The staff is notably attentive and genuinely skilled at guiding guests toward choices that match individual preferences.
